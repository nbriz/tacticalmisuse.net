this site [will] contains a collection of creative && experimental ways of using the browser + other Internet tech to engage w/online platforms in a manner that undermines the exploitative algorithms + dark patterns designed by the data barons of surveillance capitalism.

the code, techniques && perspectives documented on this site are influenced by various other artists, activists && hackers from various communities i've been actively involved in over the years including glitch art, copyleft/open-source/remix culture, net.art, web development && cybersecurity.

i'm still in the process of documenting these techniques, but in the meantime u can watch my 2018 talk an Artist's Guide To Tactical Mis-Use at the FRNAK conference in Gainsville, FL, or my 2017 talk Their Tools, Ur Rules: The Tactical Mis-Use of Online Platforms; at the Radical Networks conference in New York City, NY.
